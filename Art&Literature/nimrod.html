Nimrod

Nimrod is the son of Cush and therefore the great-grandson of Noah. He is described as the king of Shinar (Mesopotamia). The Bible states that he was a "mighty hunter before the Lord [and] ... began to be mighty in the earth." Later extra-biblical traditions identified Nimrod as the ruler who commissineed the construction of the Tower of Babel, which led to his reputaion as a king who was rebellious against God.
